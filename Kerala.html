Welcome to Kerala: God's Own Country

Day 1: Arrival in Kochi

Morning: Arrive in Kochi, the vibrant gateway to Kerala. Check into your hotel and freshen up.

Afternoon: Explore the historic Fort Kochi, visiting St. Francis Church, the oldest European church in India, and the Jewish Synagogue.

Evening: Enjoy a seafood dinner at a restaurant overlooking the Chinese Fishing Nets, a unique sight not to be missed.

Day 2: Backwaters Cruise in Alleppey

Morning: Embark on a backwaters cruise in Alleppey, gliding through serene canals and experiencing the tranquil beauty of Kerala's waterways.

Afternoon: Witness the traditional Kettuvallam boats, palm-fringed landscapes, and local villages along the backwaters.

Evening: Relax on the deck of your houseboat, enjoying the sunset and the sounds of nature.

Day 3: Exploring Munnar

Morning: Visit the Munnar hill station, nestled amidst rolling hills and lush tea plantations.

Afternoon: Take a scenic drive through the tea plantations and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding hills.

Evening: Relax at your resort with a cup of freshly brewed Munnar tea, soaking in the cool mountain air.

Day 4: Jeep Safari and Kathakali Performance

Morning: Embark on a jeep safari through the Eravikulam National Park, spotting wildlife like Nilgiri Tahr, elephants, and birds.

Afternoon: Visit the Echo Point and enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and hills.

Evening: Experience the warmth of a traditional Kathakali performance, showcasing vibrant costumes, storytelling through dance, and intricate makeup.

Day 5: Wildlife Adventure in Thekkady

Morning: Travel to Thekkady, a haven for wildlife enthusiasts.

Afternoon: Take a boat ride on Periyar Lake, spotting wildlife like elephants, tigers, and various bird species.

Evening: Witness a breathtaking Kalaripayattu performance, a traditional martial art form of Kerala.

Day 6: Spice Plantations and Ayurvedic Massage

Morning: Visit the spice plantations of Kumarakom, learning about the cultivation and processing of spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves.

Afternoon: Enjoy a traditional Ayurvedic massage, a rejuvenating experience based on ancient Indian principles.

Evening: Take a sunset cruise on Vembanad Lake, witnessing the vibrant hues of the sky reflected on the water.

Day 7: Relaxing in Kovalam

Morning: Drive to Kovalam, a renowned beach destination with pristine beaches and laid-back vibes.

Afternoon: Relax on the beach, soaking up the sun and enjoying the soothing sound of the waves.

Evening: Take a dip in the Arabian Sea and enjoy a delicious seafood dinner on the beach.

Day 8: Temples and Museums in Thiruvananthapuram

Morning: Visit the Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Thiruvananthapuram, a magnificent temple complex dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Afternoon: Explore the Napier Museum, showcasing a collection of archaeological artifacts, bronzes, and traditional Kerala art.

Evening: Enjoy a performance of Mohiniyattam, a graceful classical dance form of Kerala.

Day 9: Backwaters Trip and Farewell Dinner

Morning: Take a boat trip to the backwaters of Alappuzha, observing the local village life and traditional fishing practices.

Afternoon: Visit the Kuttanad region, known as the "rice bowl of Kerala," and witness the unique rice farming techniques.

Evening: Enjoy a farewell dinner at a traditional Kerala restaurant, savoring local delicacies like Appam and Meen Curry.

Day 10: Departure from Kerala

Morning: Depart from Kerala, carrying memories of your unforgettable backwaters, spice-filled adventures, and the beauty of the "God's Own Country."

Note: This itinerary is just a suggestion and can be adapted to your interests and preferences. There are countless other destinations and activities to explore in Kerala, so feel free to create your own unique and memorable travel experience.
